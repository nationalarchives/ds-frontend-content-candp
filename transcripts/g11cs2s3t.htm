Wandsworth Council for Community Relations sub-
mitted evidence to the Select Committee which reported that
'…many West Indians in Wandsworth mistrust the police
and that the youth regard the police as enemies.' (7) A
Liverpool youth club worker claimed that 'In the eyes of the
coloured youngsters the police are seen as an entirely destruc-
tive group of people.' (8) And when the Committee visited a
centre in Notting Hill a group of young immigrants told them
that they and their friends '…have been treated really badly
by the police and naturally they hate them.' (9)
