(ADM 101/21/7)

Journal of a voyage from England to New South
Wales in the Earl St Vincent Convict Ship

Patrick Hill Surgeon & Superintendent.

1820(March) 28th Ship came into Spithead, moderate breezes from ws wIn the morning inspected 60 Convicts on board the Laurelat 1 Pm Received 160 Convicts on board, Twenty of whomare boys under 18 years of age, who have a separate prison

Sunday May 7th
Moderate breezes and fine weather. Bedding on deck.-
A division admitted to Bathe at 6 am - Prison day -
holystoned - at 11 all the Convicts on deck attending Divine
Service, the Guard and Crew likewise present.-
Inspected the Convicts, found them all clean.
