(SP 16/270) (50)

At bottom corner of page:
To Alexander Baker Esq. &
Sergiant Clowes; his Majesties
Chirurgions.

Theise shalbe to will & require you forthwith to make
choise of such Midwives as you shall thinke best
to inspect & search the Boddies of those Women that
were lately brought up by the Sheriff of the Court
of Lancaster indicted for Witchcraft, and to report
unto you whether they finde about them any such
markes as are pretended; Wherein thesaid Midwives
are to receave instructions from Mr Doctor Harvy
his Majesties Phisitian & your selves: Dated at Whyte :
hall the 29th of June 1634.
