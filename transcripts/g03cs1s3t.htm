a. (membrane 27)


March 10. General pardon to John de Kirkton, knight, who lately aided the king
Chertsey. when he took the field against certain rebels. By p.s.

The like to the following:-
John son of John le Warenner. Eustace de Folvill.
John son of Thomas Wygge. Laurence de Folevill.
Robert de Folevill. Walter son of Peter Echard.
Walter de Folevill. John de Driby.
John Rydel of Petham.
