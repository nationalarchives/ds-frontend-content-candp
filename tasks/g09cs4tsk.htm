Case-Study 4: Were the Victorians cruel to children who broke the law?
 

Discussion Points:
1. Look at the sources to see how children were regarded and treated by the law in Victorian Britain. In what ways are things different now? Do you think we have anything to learn from what the sources tell us?
2. Should children be sent to adult prisons? At what age does a child become an adult, as far as crime is concerned?
