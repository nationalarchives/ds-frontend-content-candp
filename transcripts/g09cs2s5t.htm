(HO44/28)

Extracts from the visiting Justices reports
relating to the System of Silence being
introduced into the House of Correction at
Northallerton

…its effects have been found
eminently beneficial, in fact all that
noise and loose and profligate
conversation amongst the prisoners, and
the consequent evil effects of contamination
which could not heretofore be prevented,
are now entirely put an end to: and
the good order and decorum, which
they have before adverted to, is very
much to be attributed to the exact and
rigid enforcement of Silence, both by
night and day.
