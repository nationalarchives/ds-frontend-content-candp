The 1957 total of all traffic offenses (271,149) is
12.8 per cent. higher than the total for 1956 (240,459).
This continues the annual upward trend which, except in
1949 and 1953, has been apparent for some years. The
figure of 12.8 per cent., which compares with 12.1 per cent.
last year, bears some relationship to the annual increase
of 10-11 per cent. in the number of motor vehicles
Licensed.
