(HO73/4)

The Report of the Police Association
at Bourton on the water in the County of
Gloucester, addressed to Mess.rs Lefeures
Rowan and Chadwick -

George Cooper George Cooper has so conducted himself during
the two years and three months of his residence
at Bourton as to have obtained the goodwill of
every description of persons - He has been vigilant
firm, obliging, and active; as judicious in suppressing
small offences as in bringing forward more important
changes - His behaviour has been so satisfactory
that voluntary donations have been given him
each year, above his regular salary -
