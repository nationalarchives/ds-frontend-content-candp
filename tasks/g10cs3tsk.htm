Case Study 3 - Juvenile Crime
 
(i) Use all the Sources to make a list of all the causes which have been put forward as a cause of juvenile crime.
(ii) Give each a mark out of 5. (5=serious, 1=unlikely).
(iii) Add any reasons you think are important causes of juvenile crime not on the list and mark them.
(iv) Compare your list and your marks with someone else in your class.
(v) Interview an adult and compare their reactions and ideas with your own. What are the key differences?.
