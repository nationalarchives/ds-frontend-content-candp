(ii)

A more structured
partnership between police
and public in crime
prevention

We have always sought public co-
operation, but in the past we have relied
largely on exhortation. Over the last four
years, however, we have been deliber-
ately organising a coherent structure
within which the public will be enabled
to work with us in a purposeful manner.
The building blocks so far are consult-
ative groups, neighbourhood watch
schemes, business watch schemes, victim
support schemes, crime prevention
panels and estate policing projects.
