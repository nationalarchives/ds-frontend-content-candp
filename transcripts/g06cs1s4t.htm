In the Queens most Excellent
Majesty

the Humble Petition of Joane
Evance wife of Robert Evance

Humbly Sheweth ~

That your Pet.ns husband Robert Evance Stands
Convicted att Worcester for Stealing a horse from one
Thomas Downe, but was repreved by the Judges to the
sixth of may next in regard it was the first crime that her
said husband ever committed

That your Pet.n has a smale Infant att her breast
who must Inevitably perish if her said husband be executed

May It Therefore please your most
Sacred Majesty according Your wonted
goodnesse and Mercy to Spare her
husbands life by granting him a
Pardon it being the fist fault that
he ever committed or was Charged
with he being willing to serve yr. Majty
by sea or land.

And Yr.Petn
Will ever pray
