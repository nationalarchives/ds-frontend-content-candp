20. Unemployment among parents - However adequately
social legislation may provide for this eventuality, a long
period of unemployment must weaken the moral fibre of most
men and tend to make them less effective leaders in the home.

Unemployment of the father has one other
effect ; when the father is out of work it not uncommonly
necessitates the mother becoming a wage-earner, with her
consequent absence from home when she is most needed there.

22. The multiple store
The multiple store with its open display of goods, which
has now become a permanent feature of English town life, is
reputed to be a hunting ground of the juvenile delinquent.

types of film, the influence of which has lately been accentu-

the sound film, which tend to promote unhealthy relations
between the sexes. They may possibly not be injurious to the

that they are a direct cause of delinquency, but there is no
doubt that they constitute an incitement to looseness of
conduct in juveniles which is subsequently reflected in a
lowering of the moral standard. It would be desirable for
the attention of the Entertainments Committee of the Council
to be drawn to these matters.
