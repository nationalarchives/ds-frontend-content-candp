(PC1/83)

554 NewSouthwales
No.7 Bedford Ave Dublin
6 Feby 1835,

Honourable Sir,

I ask pardon for the liberty I
take in addressing you in the hope that
you would be pleased to let me know
whether My Son: George Bonfield is living
or not he left this City under a Rule of
Transportation for the feloney of a Sid Board
and sailed from Kingstown Harbour in July
1830, on Board the Ship Hercules Cap.tn Vaughan
Since which time I never heard from him but
wonce which was shortly after his Arrival
in 1831.
