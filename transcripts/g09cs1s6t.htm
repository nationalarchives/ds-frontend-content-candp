TO THE
QUEEN'S MOST EXCELLENT MAJESTY.

THE PETITION OF THE COLONISTS OF NEW SOUTH WALES, IN PUBLIC
MEETING ASSEMBLED, SIGNED BY THE PRESIDENT AND COUNCIL OF
THE AUSTRALASIAN LEAGUE, ON BEHALF OF THE MEETING,

(text omitted)

That, by an address from the Legislative Council of New South Wales to His Excellency the Governor General, / adopted the 14th September, 1847, having reference to a Despatch from Your Majesty's Principal Secretary of State for the / Colonies, touching the Renewal of Transportation to this Colony, the opinion of the Council was recorded, "That a Return to /
"the system of Transportation would be opposed to the wishes of the community, and would be most injurious to the social, / "moral, and political advancement of the Colony."

(Left margin note)

14th Sept., 1847.
