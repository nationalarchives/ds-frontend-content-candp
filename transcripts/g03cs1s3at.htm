(membrane 44d. )

Feb. 18.
Kennington The like of Thomas Wake of Lydell, Nicholas de Cantilupo,
William de Eyncourt and Robert de Colvill to take William de Sudbury, knight,
William de Burton, knight, and Richard de Folvill, parson of the church
of Teye, and imprison them in the Tower of London in the custody of the
constable. By C. in Parliament.
