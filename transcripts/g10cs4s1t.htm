SECRET

The following are the main points that I have elicited: -

(a) Many of the more senior officers who have been
brought up in the old school are still using
their influence to water down and suppress
strict reporting when there is the slightest
loophole.

(text omitted)

(b) Suppression chiefly occurs after the crime has
been briefly reported in the crime book and
is entrusted to subordinate C.I.D. officers to
investigate. The case is watered down if
possible at the outset and no crime report
is prepared. Subordinate C.I.D. officers
are encouraged to do this by their seniors
who wish to shew as little crime as possible.
